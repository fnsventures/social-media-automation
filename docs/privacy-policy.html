Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Social Media Automation tool used by Bishnupriya Fuels to publish content to Facebook and Instagram.

What we collect

The app uses Meta (Facebook/Instagram) APIs only to publish posts on pages you authorize. We do not sell personal data. Access tokens and account IDs are stored as encrypted GitHub Secrets or local environment variables, not in public code.

How we use data

Credentials are used solely to publish scheduled social media content to your connected Facebook Page and Instagram Business account.

Data retention

Post content is stored in this project repository. API tokens remain until you revoke them in Meta or remove GitHub Secrets.
